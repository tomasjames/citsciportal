Citizen Science?

We would like people to engage in interesting and potentially exciting investigations. You could be a school or university student, an amateur astronomer, or just someone with an interest in being involved in science. We think of you all as Citizen Scientists.

We are developing more ways for you to be involved in science investigations which will make use of our robotic telescope network. In the meantime our website has lots of resources for you to explore.

Website Resources

These are some of the resources you can download and use from our website, without taking part in a project or observing campaign
Observations
Browse our archive of observations, download the astronomical data files and images, view the images as a slideshow and more.
Virtual Sky
Embed our planetarium in your website or blog. Customize it to any date and viewing location on Earth. Show constellations, planets, and a variety of different sky projections.
Activities
Browse through some of the things you can do with archive data and without a telescope.
Education website
Have a look at the education and public engagement side of LCOGT.
